How our pricing works

Our estimator gives you a real labor cost range based on your project type, material grade, and square footage. No tricks, no bait-and-switch.

Material costs are estimated based on grade selection. Final pricing confirmed on your free in-home walkthrough — but most jobs land within 10% of this estimate.

Refinishing is labor-only pricing — supply costs are minimal and included in the per-foot rate.

New hardwood and LVP estimates include both labor and a material range based on your grade selection.
